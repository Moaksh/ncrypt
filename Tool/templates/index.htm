About the app

Between 2000 and 2019, the number of people with electricity increased from 78 to 87 percent, and the numbers without electricity dipped to just below one billion. Yet, as the population continues to grow, so will the demand for cheap energy, and an economy reliant on fossil fuels is creating drastic changes to our climate. Thus, investing in alternate and natural sources and ensuring energy for all is vital.

With one goal in mind- ‘to ensure reliable and continuous power supply and to provide electricity to each and every household’ we, at URJA have developed an innovative way to generate electricity and light up our homes from urine, creating an affordable, renewable and carbon-neutral way of generating power.
